Glenorchy City Council's GIS Mapping and Data

Glenorchy is a local government in the north of Greater Hobart in Tasmania.

This website is a place where you can view maps and access data that Council looks after.

Please take the time to look around and drop us an email if you like it, or even if you don't. The only way it will get better is with your feedback and through requests. Please contact us.

Licensing

To read about the licence under which we share information, see here: Glenorchy City Council GIS data licensing. Most data available through this website via vector based web services is available under the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Australia licence. There are basemaps that use state government data, and some apps (like the planning scheme maps) are based on data that we can't share. But where we can, we want to allow you to do what you want with the data. Hopefully that's useful and we can build great things together!
