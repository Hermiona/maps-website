Glenorchy City Council's GIS Mapping and Data

This website is the beginnings of a place where you can view maps and access data that the Council look after.

Although only beginning, please take the time to look around and drop us an email if you like it, or even if you don't. The only way it will get better is with your feedback and through requests. Please contact us.
